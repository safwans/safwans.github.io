Description

MEET RIVERSTONE APARTMENTS - Luxury North Macon apartments voted Macon's Best of the Best Apartments by the Telegraph! Our gated community is conveniently located in North Macon, with quick and easy access to Interstate 75. Our one, two, and three bedroom spacious apartments feature open layouts, nine-foot ceilings, ceiling fans, gourmet kitchens, espresso cabinetry, oversized closets, garden tubs, and plantation-style blinds. RiverStone residents come home to all-inclusive amenities including a resort-style saltwater pool, fitness center, clubhouse social room, pet park, car care center, 24/7 business center, grilling stations and so much more! If you're looking for a great North Macon apartment, we'd love to show you why RiverStone is the place to be. Stop by for a tour today - We'd love to meet you. Apply today!
